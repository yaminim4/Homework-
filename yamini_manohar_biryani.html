Biryani

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Biryani is a flavored rice made with meat, vegetables and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 medium red onion, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 black cloves
  • 1 star anise
  • 2 cardamoms
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 green chillies, finely chopped
  • 1 small sprig of mint, destemmed and minced
  • chopped, assorted vegetables
  • 1 cup of steamed chicken
  • 2 cups rice - washed and soaked
  • 1 cup cocunut milk
  • 2 tsp lemon juice

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large pressure cooker.
  2. Add spices and sautee until slightly browned.
  3. Add sliced onion and ginger garlic paste. Sautee on medium flame.
  4. Add chopped chillies, mint leaves, vegetables, and chicken. Tumble in spices.
  5. Add washed rice, cocunut milk, and 1 cup of water.
  6. Add the lemon juice, and close the pressure cooker. Allow three whistles before turning off the flame.
  7. Let the pressure cooker cool slightly before opening.
  8. Serve hot, with a garnish of fresh mint leaves.
